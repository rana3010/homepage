Useful and forbidden food for cats!

Useful food:

  • Fruits
    • Banana - Watermelon Blueberry - Cantaloupe - Pumpkin
  • Vegetables
    • Cucumber without seeds - Spinach peas - Fries without peel
  • Dairy and eggs
    • Yogurt and Nesto Cheese (once a day) - Boiled eggs (not much)
  • Meat and poultry
    • Chicken - Turkey - Liver and Meat (well cooked) - Fish (twice a month)

Forbidden food:

  • Drinks
    • Alcohol - Caffeine - Milk - Soft drinks
  • Sweets
    • Sugar - Chocolate - Gum - Ice cream - Raisins - Nuts
  • Fruits and Vegetables
    • Garlic - onion - tomato - grape - avocado
  • Dairy products
    • Milk (causes stomach upset)!!.
  • Meat and poultry
    • Bone (in all its forms) - Meat and Fish (uncooked) - fats
  • Other foods
    • Chipsy - Salt - Salty food - Human drugs without consulting the veterinarian

Do not weaken!
